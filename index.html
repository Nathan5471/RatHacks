About Rat Hacks

What

Rat Hacks is a hackathon hosted by the Roanoke Valley Govenor's School (RVGS). Rat Hacks will take place on May 24, 2025 from 8AM to 8PM. It will be hosted at 2104 Grandin Road SW, Roanoke, VA 24015 (the RVGS building)

Teams

Teams can be made of 1 to 4 people. You can form your own team or we can help you find one.

Applying

Rat Hacks is open to any high school student. You can find the application here.

Prizes

Prizes will be awarded to the winning teams of Rat Hacks. More information will be shared later.

Food & Drinks

We will have options such as pizza, chicken nuggets, and water. If you have any dietary restrictions, please let us know on your application.

Judging

Judging will happen during the week following the event. There will be a virtual awards ceremony hosted on the following Saturday: May 31st, 2025. A scoring sheet will be shared before the hackathon.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where hackers (thats you!) work in teams to develop a project for a set period of time. There is no actual hacking in the illegal sense, just people developing a project.

When is Rat Hacks?

Rat Hacks is on May 24, 2025 from 8AM to 8PM.

What do I need to bring?

You can bring your own laptop and charger, but we will have some that you can use. If you plan on using one of ours, please mark that option on the registration form.

How much does this cost?

Rat Hacks is free to attend!

What if I have more questions?

You can email us at questions@rathacks.com
